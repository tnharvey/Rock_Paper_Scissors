Let's Play Rock Paper Scissors!
This is the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors. If (by some miracle) you have never played it, the rules are simple:
  1. Choose rock, paper, or scissors.
  2. Compare your choice to what your opponent chose, using the chart to the right.
  3. Somebody wins! First to 5 points wins the match!
Round winner will be indicated by a golden bar beneath their choice. Have fun!
